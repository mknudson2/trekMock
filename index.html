Our mission

Trek started in a small Wisconsin barn in 1976, but our founders always saw somthing bigger. Decades later, we're on a mission to make our world a better place to live and ride.

We only build products we love, provide incredible hospitality to our customers, and change the world by getting more people on bikes.
